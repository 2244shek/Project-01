Our Story

About Art InGene

A one-stop hub for every tool you need, born from a student's weekend project.

Art InGene is a curated collection of the best free online tools for learning, development, creativity, resume building, and daily tasks — built so you never have to hunt across a dozen tabs again. 💝

Timeline

The Journey 🎭

1

18 February 2022

The Spark

On a fine evening, the idea of building a single website to house all the best free tools was born. After learning web development from YouTube, I felt motivated to build my very first self-employed project. The first step was designing the site's wireframe in Whimsical, followed by coding out the landing page in HTML.

2

1 March 2022

Styling with TailwindCSS

Once the HTML structure was in place, I discovered TailwindCSS — a utility-first CSS framework that made styling significantly faster. I completed the Tailwind tutorial in two weeks and began applying it across the project. The goal was simple: finish the project, boost confidence, and stay consistent.

3

7 March 2022

The Classic Struggle ☠️

Like most beginners, I ran into the inevitable "break in consistency." Life, studies, and other commitments created gaps — but the project was never abandoned. Every setback taught a lesson about persistence and the long game of improving as a developer.

4

2024 — Redesign

A Fresh Look

Years later, the site gets a complete design overhaul — dark mode, responsive layout, unified component system, and a cleaner structure. The tools remain, the mission stays the same, and the journey continues.
